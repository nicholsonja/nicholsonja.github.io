A brief list of generative A.I. resources

Image generation

  • DALL·E 2

    https://openai.com/product/dall-e-2
    You get 15 free credits that replenish monthly. Free credits don’t roll over to the next month, so they expire a month after they were granted - but you'll get 15 new free credits.

    DALL·E 2 Gallery on Instagram

  • Midjourney

    https://www.midjourney.com
    Generating an image activates the free Midjourney trial. Trial users can make roughly 25 Jobs before needing to subscribe. Requires a Discord login, which is free.

    Midjourney Community Showcase

  • Stable Diffusion

    https://huggingface.co/spaces/stabilityai/stable-diffusion
    A basic, demo interface. No account required.

    https://beta.dreamstudio.ai
    DreamSpace (beta), a more advanced interface. Limited free credits, but more can be purchased. Requires a free account.

    Midjourney Community Showcase

Text generation

  • ChatGPT

    https://openai.com/blog/chatgpt
    Free, but you need to create an account.

  • GPT-4 in Bing

    http://bing.com/new
    Sign up for the new Bing preview. Free, but requires a Microsoft account.

  • Bard

    https://bard.google.com/
    Join the wait-list. Free, but requires a Google account.
